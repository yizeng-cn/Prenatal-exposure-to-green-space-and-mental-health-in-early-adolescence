# Prenatal-exposure-to-green-space-and-mental-health-in-early-adolescence
> **Note:** These are codes of analyses for the study "Prenatal exposure to green space and mental health in early adolescence: findings from the TRAILS study" (https://doi.org/10.1093/aje/kwae373)

> Three Mplus files are uploaded: 1) Multiple imputation (impute); 2) Main analysis (path_impute); 3) Complete-case analysis (complete)

## Variable descriptions
* Outcomes
  - y1-y4: Externalising problems, internalising problems, tobacco use, alcohol use

* Mediators
  - m1: gestational age, m2: birth weight

* Individual- and family-level covariates
  - p1: family SES
  - p2: parental externalising problems
  - p3: parental internalising problems
  - p4: maternal age at childâ€™s birth
  - p5: maternal tobacco use
  - p6: maternal alcohol use
  - p7: all complications (except preterm birth and low/over birth weights)
  - p8: age
  - p9: sex
  - p10: ethnicity
  - p11: parental divorce

* Area-level covariates
  - n1: Prenatal exposure to socioeconomic deprivation
  - n2: Prenatal exposure to social fragmentation
  - n4: Exposure to socioeconomic deprivation in early adolescence
  - n5: Exposure to social fragmentation in early adolescence
  - n3a: Prenatal exposure to urbanicity
  - n6a: Exposure to urbanicity in early adolescence
  - n7a: Prenatal exposure to PM2.5
  - n8a: Exposure to PM2.5 in early adolescence
  - n9: Prenatal exposure to noise
  - n10: Exposure to noise in early adolescence

* Exposures
  - x1, x2, xc: prenatal green space exposure, current green space exposure, green space change
